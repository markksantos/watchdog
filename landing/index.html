Features

Built for quiet protection

Powerful monitoring packed into a tiny menu bar icon. No windows, no clutter — just intelligent security.

Face Detection

Powered by Apple's Vision framework. Detects faces in real time with zero cloud dependency — everything stays on your Mac. Know the instant someone appears at your desk.
